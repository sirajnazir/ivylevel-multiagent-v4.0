- Paragraph 2: My solution through Empowering AI (SOLUTION)
- Paragraph 3: More problems with AI algorithms (PROBLEM)
- Paragraph 4: More solutions (SOLUTION)

**Jenny's Diagnosis:** "This doesn't make sense‚Äîyou're talking about misrepresentation, then you talk about your solution, then you go back to talking about more problems. Should be issues then solutions."

### The Solution: Consolidation

**New Structure:**
1. **ALL PROBLEMS FIRST**
   - General: AI misrepresentation exists
   - Specific: Discriminatory algorithms, biased image generation
   - Personal: My hackathon experience feeling unsafe

2. **THEN ALL SOLUTIONS**
   - What I'm doing: Empowering AI nonprofit
   - Impact achieved: Workshops, curriculum development
   - Future vision: Broader change goals

### Why This Works
- **Logical Flow**: Build the problem completely before solving
- **Emotional Arc**: Accumulate weight before hope
- **Clear Narrative**: Reader journey from problem to solution
- **Professional**: Standard problem-solution structure

### The Restructuring Process
1. Identify paragraph out of place
2. Remove it from current position
3. Move ALL problems to beginning
4. Group ALL solutions together
5. Check transitions between sections
