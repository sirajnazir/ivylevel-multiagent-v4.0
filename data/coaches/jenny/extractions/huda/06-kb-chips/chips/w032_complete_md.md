## ‚öñÔ∏è The Give-and-Get Framework

### The Problem
Most students only write about what they want to receive.

### The Insight
"Tons of people are interested...but what do YOU have to contribute?"

### The Framework

**Part 1: What You'll Get**
- Technical skills (Python programming, AI algorithms)
- Network (peers, faculty, industry connections)
- Independence (first time extended period away from home)

**Part 2: What You'll Give**
- Diverse perspective (computer science AND media arts background)
- Specific projects (game development, film club leadership)
- Enthusiasm (excitement to share in classroom discussions)

### Application to CMU Essay

**Before (GET only):**
"I hope to gain technical skills in AI and meet other students interested in technology."

**After (GET + GIVE):**
"I hope to deepen my technical skills while contributing my unique perspective at the intersection of computer science and media arts. Having led my school's film club and developed games, I'm excited to share these interdisciplinary experiences in classroom discussions and collaborate with peers who bring different strengths."

### Why It Matters
Shows admissions you understand it's a community, not a vending machine.
